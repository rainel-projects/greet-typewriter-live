Letters Written in Moonlight

Whispered through constellations,
inked upon the breath of stars,
a letter drifts between souls unseen.

Beneath the quiet hum of the sky,
dreams gather like dew on midnight leaves.
Every word stitched with stardust,
folded gently into dawn.
